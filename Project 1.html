Twice Baked Potato Recipe

This recipe is a great one to make any guests happy, or just you and some friends. Its more simple to make than you might think.

Ingredients

  • 6 Russet Potatos (Scrubbed Clean)
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • 1/2 c milk
  • 1/2 c sour cream
  • 1/2 c shredded cheddar
  • 2 scallions thinly sliced for garnish

Directions

For Oven

Step 1

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F.
  2. Pat potatos completly dry with paper towels.
  3. Poke potatos all over with a fork, then rub with oil and sprinkle with salt
  4. Place potatos directly on oven racks and bake until easily pierced with a fork (about an hour)
  5. Place on a baking sheet and let cool until cool enough to handle.

Step 2

  1. Cut a thin layer off the top of each potato
  2. Scoop inside out of each potato, leaving a 1/2 border
  3. Place insides in a large bowl
  4. Keep the potato tops and roast them on the tray as a snack

Step 3

  2. Mash until butter is melted and potatos are mostly smooth, with some chunks
  3. Add 1 Cup cheddar and scallions and stir until incorporated
  4. Season with salt and pepper

Step 4

  1. Fill baked potatos with potato mixture, and place on large baking sheet
  2. Top with remaining 1/2 cup cheddar
  3. Bake until cheese is melty and outside is crispy (About 15 Minutes

Step 4

Garnish with scallions before serving and enjoy!
